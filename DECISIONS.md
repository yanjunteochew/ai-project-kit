# Decision Log

## ADR-0001 — AI Project Kit uses an AI-first, Human-led workflow

**Date:** 2026-07-17

**Status:** Accepted

### Decision

AI Project Kit will be designed around an AI-first, Human-led engineering workflow.

### Context

The objective of AI Project Kit is to improve collaboration between humans and AI coding assistants while ensuring humans retain ownership of product vision and technical decisions.

### Alternatives Considered

- Human-only development
- Fully autonomous AI development

### Rationale

A collaborative model combines human judgement with AI productivity while maintaining accountability and code quality.

### Consequences

Positive:
- Clear ownership
- Repeatable workflow
- Better maintainability

Negative:
- Requires discipline
- Documentation becomes an essential part of development
